ed tovell dot com

Music is very important to Ed Tovell's life and he is constantly singing and writing music and going to watch and listen to music being played by other people.

Ed Tovell and his girlfriend are in a band called Platypus Bear. They both sing and play all the instruments. They record demos and put them online as EPs every now and then.

They also play live a lot. Here they are in the Bloomsbury Festival in 2012.

Ed Tovell also used to play bass in the Tin Can .44s, and has been involved in numerous electronic/ambient/terrible experiemtental side-projects with various North London hipsters.
